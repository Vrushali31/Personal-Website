About Me

Hello viewers! I am Vrushali Ranadive. I am a young dynamic woman born and raised in Vadodara, Gujarat, India. I believe in learning new skills and have always been fascinated by advancement of technology. Apart from my passion for Computer Science, I also love sports, dancing and music.
